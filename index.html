About Me

Hello! I’m Sandip Khadka


I am an Information Security Analyst with hands-on experience in vulnerability management, threat detection, and endpoint security across enterprise environments. With a strong foundation in cybersecurity operations, network troubleshooting, and secure remote support, I bring a detail-oriented and solution-focused approach to improving organizational security and supporting incident response workflows.
